# Detector

A browser extension that lets you download videos and photos from your favorite websites. Works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers.

## What Can It Do?

- Works with over 150 websites including YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, and more
- Handles both regular video files and HLS/M3U8 streaming formats
- Lets you choose video quality when multiple options are available
- Shows real-time download progress so you know what's happening

## Installation

1. Open your browser and navigate to `chrome://extensions/` (or `edge://extensions/` for Edge)
2. Turn on "Developer mode" using the toggle in the top-right corner
3. Click "Load unpacked" and select the Detector folder from where you downloaded it
4. You'll see the Detector icon appear in your browser toolbar

## How to Use

1. Visit any supported website like YouTube, Instagram, or Twitter
2. Click the Detector icon in your browser toolbar
3. The extension will show you all available videos and photos on the page
4. Simply click the download button next to the content you want to save

## Supported Websites

Detector works with a wide range of popular platforms including YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, Facebook, Reddit, Twitch, Vimeo, Dailymotion, and over 150 more sites.
